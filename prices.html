Lesson Packages

All of ELTS payments are dealt with through Paypal. You can pay for your lessons using a debit/credit card (e.g. VISA, Mastercard) or Paypal account. Paypal is the world’s most popular online payment system and is both safe and convenient.

PLEASE NOTE: You have to contact ELTS first to undertake your trial consultation, choose your teacher and accept our terms and conditions BEFORE you purchase your lesson package.

How to Pay

  • Select the lesson package you would like to buy
  • Click on the Buy Package button
  • Continue to Checkout to confirm your purchase
  • You will receive confirmation emails from both PayPal and ELTS after successfully completing the purchase

*IMPORTANT: ELTS only accepts payments in GBP. If your bank account is in another currency, Paypal may charge 2.5% commission for currency conversion to GBP.
