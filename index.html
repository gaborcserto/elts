Exam Preparation

Whether you are taking the TOEFL, IELTS or another of the Cambridge exams, give yourself the best chance by preparing with our ELTS experts

Conversational English

At ELTS we realise that exposure to native English speakers and the opportunity to speak is vital for your English language to development. ELTS provides intelligent and focused conversational courses that simultaneously improve your fluency and turn your passive vocabulary to active

Business English Courses

ELTS Business English training courses are specifically designed around you by our qualified native teachers. Our to courses can be arranged at any time of day to suit your busy schedule.

Academic English

ELTS Academic English courses enable you to achieve your educational aims and goals. Our British and US teachers provide you with all the necessary skills to be successful in your academic endeavours and ensure you can achieve the best results possible.

ESP

ELTS provides profession specific courses which are focused on increasing and developing your English expertise within your field of expertise, enabling you to speak with greater fluency and confidence at work

UK based ESOL

ELTS ESOL courses are all incredibly practical and consider the realities and challenges of living in the UK and USA to give you the language and cultural knowledge to succeed in your career, studies and social life.

Conversation Clubs

ELTS Conversation Clubs give you the opportunity to discuss business, political, scientific and societal topics with between 3-4 other students via Skype, and are overseen by of our expert teachers whom act as the Chairperson for each discussion.

Lesson Packages
